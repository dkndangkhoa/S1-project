VR CS-GO

Time: 19:20
Place: Rachelsmolen, Eindhoven
Price: €10


The VR CS-GO in a suit gaming event is a high-energy, action-packed experience that combines the popular first-person shooter game with the excitement of VR technology.

Attendees will have the chance to suit up in advanced VR gear and enter the virtual world of CS-GO, where they will compete against other players in fast-paced, tactical battles. With a focus on teamwork and strategy, this event is perfect for fans of CS-GO and other FPS games.

Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, the VR CS-GO in a suit gaming event is sure to provide an unforgettable gaming experience.

Event Map

Welcome to our gaming event! We're excited to have you here and look forward to providing you with an unforgettable experience.

To enter the event, please proceed to the main entrance located on the left side of the building. Once inside, you'll find the registration desk where you can pick up your tickets and get all the information you need to make the most of your time here.

Once you've got your tickets, you'll have access to all the gaming stations and areas. You'll find all the games you can play in the main hall, where you'll also find the dance floor with AR. If you need a break from gaming, you can relax and grab a drink at the bar area located on the right side of the building.

If you have any questions or need assistance during the event, please don't hesitate to ask one of our staff members, who will be happy to help. We hope you have a great time and enjoy the event to the fullest!
