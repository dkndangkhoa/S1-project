Privacy Policy

Unio Room is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and event attendees. We collect personal information, such as names and email addresses, from those who sign up for our events through our website. This information is used solely for the purpose of organizing and managing the event, and will not be shared with any third parties without your consent.

We also use cookies and other technologies to improve the user experience on our website and to track website usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may impact the functionality of the website.

Cleanliness + Hygiene

Unio Room is committed to providing a clean and hygienic environment for all attendees at our events. We understand the importance of maintaining a clean and safe environment, especially during the current global situation. We will take necessary steps to ensure that the venue, equipment, and devices are properly cleaned and disinfected before, during, and after the event.

Our staff will follow all recommended guidelines for cleaning and disinfecting surfaces. We will also wipe down all devices and equipment after each use to ensure that they are clean and safe for the next attendee.

We ask that attendees also do their part in maintaining a clean and hygienic environment by practicing good personal hygiene, wearing masks, and following social distancing guidelines.
