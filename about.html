About Us

Welcome to our website, where we aim to provide a one-stop shop for all your gaming event needs. Our team consists of Sergio, Denson, Artur, and Khoa, students of Fontys. We understand the importance of being able to attend and enjoy gaming events, which is why we have created this platform to facilitate attending gaming events and renting out gaming equipment.
Our website offers a wide range of gaming event, from small, local tournaments to large-scale events. We also have a variety of gaming equipment available for rent, including VR headsets, consoles, and more. We understand that gaming can be an expensive hobby, which is why we strive to make it more accessible by offering affordable rental options.
We are dedicated to providing a seamless and enjoyable experience for our customers. Our team is always available to assist with any questions or concerns you may have. We look forward to helping you attend and enjoy your next gaming event. Thank you for choosing us.
